Quick answer

If a dishwasher detergent pod is not dissolving, first check whether a large dish blocked the dispenser door, whether the pod was loaded into a damp cup, and whether the spray arms can move and spray normally. Pods that still stay intact after those corrections may point to cool wash water, weak circulation, or a dispenser problem that routine maintenance cannot fully solve.
