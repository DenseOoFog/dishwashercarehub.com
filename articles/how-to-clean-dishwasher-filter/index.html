How to Clean a Dishwasher Filter in 10 Minutes

Fast answer

Quick answer

Dishwasher filter cleaning is usually routine maintenance: remove food debris, rinse with warm water, brush off film gently, and reinstall the filter exactly as the manual shows.

Prep checklist

What you will need

  • owner's manual
  • dish towel
  • warm water
  • soft brush
  • sink access

Avoidable issues

Avoid these mistakes

  • Using a sharp pick that tears mesh or cracks plastic.
  • Reinstalling the filter loosely after cleaning.
  • Skipping the filter well where trapped debris causes odor and drainage issues.
