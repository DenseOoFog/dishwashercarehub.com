Quick answer

If the dishwasher soap dispenser is not opening, first check whether a tall dish blocks the door, whether sticky detergent buildup is slowing the latch, and whether the cup can open and close freely by hand. When the dispenser still fails after cleaning, drying, and better loading, the next issue may be a worn latch, spring, or control-driven release problem.
