Fast answer

Quick answer

When the dishwasher top rack is not getting clean, start by checking upper spray-arm holes, the loading pattern around cups and bowls, and lower-rack items that block the water path upward. If top-rack performance stays poor after cleaning the filter and restoring clear spray-arm movement, the problem may involve the feed tube, rack connection, or circulation system.
