Monthly dishwasher care plan

Monthly dishwasher care plan

  1. Check the filter area weekly if you run frequent loads and remove visible food soil.
  2. Wipe the door gasket, lower door edge, and detergent area every month.
  3. Inspect spray arms for debris and confirm they spin freely during routine cleaning.
  4. Run a labeled dishwasher-cleaning cycle as needed and reassess before escalating to repair.
